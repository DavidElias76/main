Welcome to Australia

Australia, a country and a continent that is surrounded by the Indian and Pacific Oceans. It is known for its unique wildlife, diverse ecosystems, and vibrant cities. The continent includes the mainland of the Australian continent, the island of Tasmania, and numerous smaller islands. Australia is notable for its high quality of life, advanced economy, and strong education system. Important Regions/Cities:
