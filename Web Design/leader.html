JOSEPH BIDEN

Joseph Robinette Biden Jr. is the 46th President of the United States, inaugurated on January 20, 2021. Born on November 20, 1942, in Scranton, Pennsylvania, he grew up in Delaware. Biden earned his law degree from Syracuse University in 1968.He was elected to the U.S. Senate in 1972, serving Delaware for 36 years. Biden was Vice President from 2009 to 2017 under President Barack Obama, focusing on economic recovery, foreign policy, and healthcare reform. In 2020, Biden defeated incumbent Donald Trump to become President. His administration has prioritized COVID-19 response, economic recovery, and climate change. Biden is married to Jill Tracy Jacobs Biden and has three children from his two marriages.
